📊 Project Overview

TransDASH is a comprehensive performance monitoring platform that provides real-time insights into key operational metrics across the nation's transit systems. The dashboard enables agencies to benchmark performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Key Features

  • Real-time performance tracking
  • Comparative agency analysis
  • Custom KPI monitoring
  • Automated data refresh
  • Interactive filtering & drill-down

💡 Impact & Results

Reduced reporting time by 60%, enabled proactive decision-making through early trend identification, and provided stakeholders with instant access to critical performance data. Used by transit agencies nationwide for strategic planning and operational improvements.
